Security Policy

1. Introduction

At Shirt Store, we are committed to protecting the security and privacy of our customers. This Security Policy outlines the measures we take to safeguard your information.

2. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption technologies to protect your personal data during transmission and storage. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

3. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account information and for all activities that occur under their account. Please notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account.

4. Incident Response

In the event of a security breach, we will promptly notify affected users and take appropriate measures to mitigate the impact. Our incident response team is trained to handle such situations effectively.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our Security Policy, please contact us at security@shirtstore.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567.
