Cancellation and Return Policy

We understand that sometimes you may need to return or cancel your order. Below are our policies for cancellations and returns:

Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel your order, please contact us within 24 hours of placing the order. Orders cannot be canceled once they have been shipped.

Return Policy

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days of receiving it. The product must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer service to initiate a return.

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned product.
